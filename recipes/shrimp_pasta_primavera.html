Shrimp Pasta Primavera

Description:

This recipe is at least 200 years old, dating back to the Middle Ages in Italy.

Despite modern modifications made to the recipe, this exact recipe has sustained the test of time and still remains the best version to date.


Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Shrimp
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Love

  1. Boil water and season the water with salt
  2. Immerse pasta into boiling water for 12 minutes
  3. While pasta is cooking, slice all the other ingredients (except love, cheese and olive oil) into tiny pieces
  4. After 12 minutes, drain the pasta
  5. Drizzle olive oil over a hot pan
  6. Stir fry the garlic
  7. Before the garlic pieces turn brown, add all the other ingredients into the pan
  8. Add the pasta into the pan
  9. Once finished stir-frying, plate the pasta and drizzle, some cheese over the hot pasta
  10. End it off with a slight dash of love over the pasta
  11. Enjoy!
